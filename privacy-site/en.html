ebookflowy Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026 · Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Clipflowy (the “Developer” or “we”) collects, uses, stores, and protects the personal information of users of the mobile application ebookflowy (the “App”). ebookflowy is a local-first e-book and document reader that stores and reads your documents on your device.

1. Summary

  • The documents you open (PDF, EPUB, TXT, HWP, etc.) and your reading progress, bookmarks, annotations, and settings are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • To serve ads (Google AdMob) and apply remote configuration (Firebase Remote Config), the App allows certain device information and advertising identifiers to be processed by third-party services.
  • We do not read or collect the contents of your documents.
  • You can control ad-tracking consent, notifications, and data deletion yourself.

2. Information We Process and Purposes

2.1 Information stored on your device (not collected by us)

ItemDescriptionStorage location
Document files and contentPDF/EPUB/TXT/Markdown/CBZ/HWP and similar files you open in the AppYour device (local)
Reading dataReading progress, bookmarks, annotations, highlights, library list, search indexYour device (local DB)
App settingsTheme, fonts, TTS and other preferencesYour device (local)

This data is kept only in your device storage and a local database (SQLite). We do not access or collect this information.

2.2 Information processed via third-party services

Third-party serviceInformation processed (examples)Purpose
Google AdMob (advertising)Advertising identifier (IDFA/AAID), coarse device information, IP address, ad-interaction dataServing and measuring in-app ads
Firebase Remote Config (Google)App instance identifier, basic technical info such as device/app versionApplying feature flags / remote configuration

Optional items operate only when you turn the corresponding feature on. If unused, the related data is not processed.

2.3 Information that may be processed automatically

The App and third-party SDKs may temporarily process general technical information such as IP address, device model, OS version, app version, and language settings to provide the service, ensure security, and diagnose errors.

3. Device Permissions

All permissions can be changed or revoked at any time in your operating system settings.

4. Advertising and App Tracking Transparency

The App displays ads via Google AdMob. On iOS, it may request your explicit consent for tracking (App Tracking Transparency); if you decline, non-personalized ads may be shown instead of personalized ones. You can manage ad personalization here:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads
  • Google Ad Settings: https://adssettings.google.com

5. Retention and Deletion

Locally stored data is removed when you delete the App or clear data within the App. Retention periods for data handled by third-party services follow those services’ policies.

6. Third-Party Privacy Policies

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob / Ads: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543
  • Firebase Privacy & Security: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not primarily directed at children under 13 (or the age defined in your country) and does not knowingly collect children’s personal information. If we learn that such information has been collected, we will delete it without undue delay.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable laws (e.g., the Korean Personal Information Protection Act, EU GDPR, US CCPA), you may request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of processing of your personal information. Requests can be sent to the contact below.

9. Security

We apply reasonable technical and administrative safeguards. Sensitive local data such as authentication tokens is stored using the operating system’s secure storage (Keychain/Keystore). However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Policy may be revised due to legal or service changes; for material changes we will provide notice via the App or this page. The revised Policy takes effect upon posting.
